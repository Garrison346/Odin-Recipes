Chicken Marsala

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Ground black pepper
  • Dried oregano
  • Medium, boneless chicken breasts
  • Butter
  • Olive oil
  • Mushrooms
  • Marsala wine
  • Cooking sherry

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the flour, salt, pepper and oregano.
  3. Coat chicken pieces in flour mixture.
  4. In a large skillet, melt butter in olive oil over medium heat.
  5. Place chicken in the pan and lightly brown.
  6. Turn over chicken pieces, and add mushrooms. Pour in wine and sherry.
  7. Cover skillet, simmer chiecken, until no longer pink.
  8. Enjoy!
