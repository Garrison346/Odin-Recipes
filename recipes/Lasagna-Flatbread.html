Lasagna Flatbread

Ingredients

  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Egg
  • Italian seasoning
  • Sausage
  • Marinara sauce
  • Flatbreads

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.
  2. Combine ricotta, mozzarella, parmesan, egg and italian seasoning in a bowl.
  3. Cook sausage until no longer pink.
  4. Stir in marinara sauce.
  5. Spread cheese mixture on flatbread.
  6. Cover with sausage.
  7. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  8. Bake until cheese is melted.
  9. Enjoy!
