Shrimp Scampi with Pasta

Ingredients

  • Shrimp
  • Pasta
  • Butter
  • Extra-virgin olive oil
  • Shallots and garlic
  • White wine
  • Lemon juice
  • Seasonings
  • Parsley

Directions

  1. Gather ingredients.
  2. Bring pot of salt water to boil, cook pasta until nearly tender.
  3. Melt butter with olive oil in large skillet.
  4. Cook and stir shallots and garlic until shallots are translucent.
  5. Season shrimp, then add shrimp to skillet until pink.
  6. Pour white wine and lemon juice into skillet and boil.
  7. Toss pasta, shrimp and parsley until mixed.
  8. Enjoy!
